The Shire-Reckoning

Welcome to the website for the ‘Shire-Reckoning’ social media accounts! Each account broadcasts the events of The Lord of the Rings on the date and time that each of them occurred:

  • Bluesky
  • Mastodon
  • Twitter

For example:

The Shire-Reckoning · Sep 22 · 5:50 am

The day dawns bright for Frodo’s 50th birthday. The only furniture remaining at Bag End are the pieces that Frodo is selling tomorrow, with the hole itself, to the Sackville-Bagginses.

This site also offers a range of older material, which was written years ago when this site offered daily summaries of The Lord of the Rings as an RSS feed:

  • An archive of the old daily summaries.
  • A description of the Shire Calendar.
  • A spectacularly thorough analysis of the Phases of the Moon in The Lord of the Rings, including every single time the phase of the Moon is mentioned by either the narrator or a character.
  • An old-school Frequently Asked Questions list: questions which, per the rules of the genre, had never actually been asked—much less ‘frequently’ asked—but were invented by the author to forestall questions that he imagined people asking.
