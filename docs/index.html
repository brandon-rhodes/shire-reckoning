The Shire-Reckoning

Welcome to the website for the ‘Shire-Reckoning’ Twitter accounts, which live-tweet the events of The Lord of the Rings on the date and time they occurred:

@ShireReckoning — Timed for Oxford, England
@ShireReckoningW — Six hours later, for the Western Hemisphere

This site also offers a range of older material, written back before Twitter existed—when this site offered daily summaries of The Lord of the Rings as an RSS feed:

  • An archive of the old daily summaries.
  • A description of the Shire Calendar.
  • A spectacularly thorough analysis of the Phases of the Moon in The Lord of the Rings, including every single time the phase of the Moon is mentioned by either the narrator or a character.
  • An old-school Frequently Asked Questions list: questions which, per the rules of the genre, had never actually been asked—much less ‘frequently’ asked—but were invented by the author to forestall questions that he imagined people asking.
